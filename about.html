About Our EV Company

Driving India towards a cleaner and smarter future.

Our Story

Eco Smart Vehicles Pvt. Ltd., established in 2016, is a leading manufacturer and solution provider in the three-wheeler electric vehicle (3W EV) segment. With a strong commitment to sustainable mobility, we specialize in designing, manufacturing, and supplying reliable, cost-effective, and high-performance electric three-wheelers for passenger and cargo applications.

Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility is located in Sonipat, Haryana, spread across 1 lakh square feet, equipped with modern assembly lines, quality control systems, and testing infrastructure. This allows us to maintain consistent product quality, scalable production, and faster delivery to our customers across India and international markets.

At Eco Smart Vehicles, we combine engineering excellence, robust supply chains and deep industry knowledge to deliver electric vehicles that are durable, efficient and tailored to real-world operating conditions. Our products are designed to offer low operating cost, high uptime and long service life, making them ideal for fleet operators, entrepreneurs and last-mile mobility providers.

Driven by innovation and customer satisfaction, we continuously invest in technology, R&D, and process improvement to stay ahead in the rapidly evolving electric mobility ecosystem.

Our Mission

To accelerate the transition to sustainable mobility by designing and manufacturing reliable, cost-effective, and high-performance electric three-wheelers that empower businesses, improve livelihoods, and reduce environmental impact.

Our Vision

We envision a future where clean electric transportation is accessible to everyone, helping cities become quieter, cleaner, and more efficient — and Eco Smart Vehicles will be at the forefront of that transformation.

Our Values

We are driven by sustainability, reliability and innovation in everything we do. Our customer-first approach ensures long-term value and dependable performance. We operate with integrity, transparency and accountability.
